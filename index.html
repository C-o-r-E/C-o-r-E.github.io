About me

I'm a Human from Montréal, Canada and have done some interesting things.

I co-founded and run Helios MakerSpace which is a non-profit community workshop. Not long after starting, people began to ask us to help build products for them. I do that too with Helios labs.

From 2012 to 2014 I was doing consulting work around the FreeRDP open source project.

Before that, I was getting my BEng in Software Engineering at Concordia University. Most of the valueable expereince I had there came from outside of class. Met a lot of great people.

Earlier than this was the era where I learned programming and a lot of computer science on my own. I did some cool reverse engineering stuff and exploited some video games.
